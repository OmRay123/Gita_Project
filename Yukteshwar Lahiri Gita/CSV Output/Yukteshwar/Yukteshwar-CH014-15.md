### Prose 
 --- 
रजसि प्रलयं गत्वा कर्मसङ्गिषु जायते |
तथा प्रलीनस्तमसि मूढयोनिषु जायते || 15||
rajasi pralayaṁ gatvā karma-saṅgiṣhu jāyate
tathā pralīnas tamasi mūḍha-yoniṣhu jāyate

### Transliteration 
 --- 
rajasi pralayam gatva karmasangishu jayate tatha pralinastamai mudhayonishu jayate - 15-

### Translation 
 --- 
No translation

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: In rajoguna, there is craving for the fruits of action. If one is immersed in tamoguna, then birth takes place in the womb of an ignorant being. - 15-
