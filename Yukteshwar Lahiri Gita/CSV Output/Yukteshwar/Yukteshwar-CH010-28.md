### Prose 
 --- 
आयुधानामहं वज्रं धेनूनामस्मि कामधुक् |
प्रजनश्चास्मि कन्दर्प: सर्पाणामस्मि वासुकि: || 28||
āyudhānām ahaṁ vajraṁ dhenūnām asmi kāmadhuk
prajanaśh chāsmi kandarpaḥ sarpāṇām asmi vāsukiḥ

### Transliteration 
 --- 
ayudhanamaham vajram dhenunamasmi kamadhuk prajanashchasmi kandarpah sarpanamasmi vasukih - 28-

### Translation 
 --- 
No translation

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: The thunderbolt among weapons, Kamadhenu among cows, Vasuki among serpents. - 28-
