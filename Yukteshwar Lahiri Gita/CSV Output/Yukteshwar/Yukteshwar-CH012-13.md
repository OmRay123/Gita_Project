### Prose 
 --- 
अद्वेष्टा सर्वभूतानां मैत्र: करुण एव च |
निर्ममो निरहङ्कार: समदु:खसुख: क्षमी || 13||
adveṣhṭā sarva-bhūtānāṁ maitraḥ karuṇa eva cha
nirmamo nirahankāraḥ sama-duḥkha-sukhaḥ kṣhamī

### Transliteration 
 --- 
adveshta sarvabhutanam maitrah karuna eva cha nirmamo nirahamkarah samaduhkhasukhah kshami - 13-

### Translation 
 --- 
No translation

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: Do not envy anyone; do not keep friendship with anyone; do not even be favorable to anyone. Nothing at all is mine; I am also nothing; knowing pleasure and sorrow [as the same], be of a forgiving nature. - 13-
