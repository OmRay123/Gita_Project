### Prose 
 --- 
इहैकस्थं जगत्कृत्स्नं पश्याद्य सचराचरम् |
मम देहे गुडाकेश यच्चान्यद्द्रष्टुमिच्छसि || 7||
ihaika-sthaṁ jagat kṛitsnaṁ paśhyādya sa-charācharam
mama dehe guḍākeśha yach chānyad draṣhṭum ichchhasi

### Transliteration 
 --- 
ihaikestham jagat kritsnam pashyadya sacharacharam mama dehe gudakesha yacchanyad drashtumicchasi - 7-

### Translation 
 --- 
No translation

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: The One Brahman is within all creation, and you are longing to see this in your own body. - 7-
