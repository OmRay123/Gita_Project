### Prose 
 --- 
श्रुतिविप्रतिपन्ना ते यदा स्थास्यति निश्चला |
समाधावचला बुद्धिस्तदा योगमवाप्स्यसि || 53||
śhruti-vipratipannā te yadā sthāsyati niśhchalā
samādhāv-achalā buddhis tadā yogam avāpsyasi

### Transliteration 
 --- 
shrutivipratipanna te yada sthasyati nishchala samadhavachala buddhistada yogamavapsyasi - 53-

### Translation 
 --- 
Secular and religious talk is of many kinds Which your confused mind hears When that buddhi is stilled in God Knowledge of All you will receive - 53-

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: The mind is confused by all kinds of secular and religious matters.  When the mind detaches itself from those things and becomes absorbed and tranquil, it becomes united with Consciousness. - 53-
