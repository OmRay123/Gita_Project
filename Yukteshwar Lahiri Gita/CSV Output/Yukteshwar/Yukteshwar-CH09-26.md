### Prose 
 --- 
पत्रं पुष्पं फलं तोयं यो मे भक्त्या प्रयच्छति |
तदहं भक्त्युपहृतमश्नामि प्रयतात्मन: || 26||
patraṁ puṣhpaṁ phalaṁ toyaṁ yo me bhaktyā prayachchhati
tadahaṁ bhaktyupahṛitam aśhnāmi prayatātmanaḥ

### Transliteration 
 --- 
patram pushpam phalam toyam yo me bhaktya prayacchati tadaham bhaktyupahritamashnami prayatatmanah - 26-

### Translation 
 --- 
One who gives leaf, flower, fruit, water as offerings Because of his devotion I receive them - 26-

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: Those who with devotion use leaf, flower, fruit, water etc. to please Atman-Consciousness - those heart-immersed sadhakas’ Spirit, being satisfied with that, manifests Itself to them. - 26-
