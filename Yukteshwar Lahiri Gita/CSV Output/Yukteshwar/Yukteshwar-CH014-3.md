### Prose 
 --- 
मम योनिर्महद् ब्रह्म तस्मिन्गर्भं दधाम्यहम् |
सम्भव: सर्वभूतानां ततो भवति भारत || 3||
mama yonir mahad brahma tasmin garbhaṁ dadhāmy aham
sambhavaḥ sarva-bhūtānāṁ tato bhavati bhārata

### Transliteration 
 --- 
mama yonirmahad brahman tasmin garbham dadhamyaham sambhavah sarvabhutanam tato bhavati bharata - 3-

### Translation 
 --- 
No translation

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: My Yoni is the Supreme Brahman. I give in that Womb, from Which everything has become. - 3-
