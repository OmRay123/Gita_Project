### Prose 
 --- 
सर्गाणामादिरन्तश्च मध्यं चैवाहमर्जुन |
अध्यात्मविद्या विद्यानां वाद: प्रवदतामहम् || 32||
sargāṇām ādir antaśh cha madhyaṁ chaivāham arjuna
adhyātma-vidyā vidyānāṁ vādaḥ pravadatām aham

### Transliteration 
 --- 
sarganamadirantashcha madhyamchaivahamarjuna adhyatmavidya vidyanam vadah pravadatamaham -32-

### Translation 
 --- 
No translation

### Commentary 
 --- 
Commentary: I am within the beginning and end of all - adhyatma vidya, meaning - the mind abiding always in Atman is what is known as vidya [knowledge]. The answer within the question. -32-
