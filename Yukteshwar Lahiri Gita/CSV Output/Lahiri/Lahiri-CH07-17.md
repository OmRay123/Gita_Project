### Prose 
 --- 
तेषां ज्ञानी नित्ययुक्त एकभक्तिर्विशिष्यते |
प्रियो हि ज्ञानिनोऽत्यर्थमहं स च मम प्रिय: || 17||
teṣhāṁ jñānī nitya-yukta eka-bhaktir viśhiṣhyate
priyo hi jñānino ’tyartham ahaṁ sa cha mama priyaḥ

### Translation 
 --- 
tesham jnani nityayukta ekabhaktirvishishyate priyo hi jnaninohtyarthamaham sa cha mama priyah

### Commentary 
 --- 
[Commentary:]18) All doors are open to him - whosoever remains stuck [or, lodged] in the par avastha of Kriya in this way - My Highest Way is verily his - within the atom in the back - moving.
