### Prose 
 --- 
अर्जुन उवाच |
कथं भीष्ममहं सङ्ख्ये द्रोणं च मधुसूदन |
इषुभि: प्रतियोत्स्यामि पूजार्हावरिसूदन || 4||
arjuna uvācha
kathaṁ bhīṣhmam ahaṁ sankhye droṇaṁ cha madhusūdana
iṣhubhiḥ pratiyotsyāmi pūjārhāvari-sūdana

### Translation 
 --- 
katham bhishmaham samkhye dronancha madhusudana ishubhih pratiyotsyami pujarharvarisudana

### Commentary 
 --- 
[Commentary:]5) Killing those whom I have perceived as good - because of attaining some future happiness - this is not correct action in my judgment.
