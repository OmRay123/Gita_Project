### Prose 
 --- 
प्रसादे सर्वदु:खानां हानिरस्योपजायते |
प्रसन्नचेतसो ह्याशु बुद्धि: पर्यवतिष्ठते || 65||
prasāde sarva-duḥkhānāṁ hānir asyopajāyate
prasanna-chetaso hyāśhu buddhiḥ paryavatiṣhṭhate

### Translation 
 --- 
prasade sarvaduhkhanam hanirasyopajayate prasannachetaso hyashu buddhih parjyavatishthate

### Commentary 
 --- 
[Commentary:]66) Without the par avastha of Kriya there is no possibility of intelligence - he is not intelligent - the one to whom the par avastha of Kriya has not happened.  He has no loving feeling; if that is not there, then there is no peace; if there is no peace, then where is happiness?
