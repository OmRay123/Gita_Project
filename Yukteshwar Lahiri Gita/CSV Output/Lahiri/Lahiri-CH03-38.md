### Prose 
 --- 
धूमेनाव्रियते वह्निर्यथादर्शो मलेन च |
यथोल्बेनावृतो गर्भस्तथा तेनेदमावृतम् || 38||
dhūmenāvriyate vahnir yathādarśho malena cha
yatholbenāvṛito garbhas tathā tenedam āvṛitam

### Translation 
 --- 
dhumenavriyate vahniryathadarsho malena cha yatholvenavrito garbhastatha tenedamavritam

### Commentary 
 --- 
[Commentary:]39) Kriyanvita people’s fire of desire is shrouded within; getting a little bit of a strong breeze - lights up immediately - and the intellect becoming ego-centric and such, is angered.
